Founding Partner

Aleksandre Kharatishvili

Licensed in New York & Georgia

Aleksandre Kharatishvili is a Fulbright Scholar and Edmund S. Muskie Professional Fellow with legal education in both the United States and Georgia. Aleksandre has worked at leading law firms, including PwC Legal in Tbilisi and Raphael Bellum PLLC in Washington, D.C., gaining extensive experience in corporate law, cross-border transactions, and complex disputes.

Education: LL.M. from Loyola University New Orleans (summa cum laude), Master of Comparative Private and International Law from New Vision University and LL.B. from Tbilisi State University (With Honours)
Bar Admissions: New York State Bar and Georgian Bar Association
Experience: 5+ years of practice in corporate, commercial, IP, immigration and dispute resolution
