Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa application process typically take? +

Processing times vary by visa type and embassy. Tourist visas typically take 7-15 business days, while work permits and residency applications can take 30-90 days. We monitor your application throughout the process and provide regular updates.

What documents are required for Georgian residency? +

Requirements include a valid passport, proof of accommodation, financial statements, health insurance, and background check certificates. Specific requirements vary by residency category. We provide a complete checklist and assist with document preparation.

Can family members be included in my application? +

Yes, spouse and minor children can typically be included in work permit and residency applications. Each family member must meet specific requirements, and additional documentation is required. We handle family immigration cases comprehensively.

What happens if my application is rejected? +

We can appeal rejections or resubmit with additional documentation addressing the concerns. Our experience helps identify potential issues early to minimize rejection risks. We provide transparent communication about realistic outcomes throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

What business structure should I choose in Georgia? +

The choice depends on your business goals, tax preferences, and liability concerns. LLCs offer flexibility and tax advantages, while corporations provide more structure for complex operations and investment. We analyze your specific situation to recommend the optimal structure.

How long does business registration take in Georgia? +

Business registration typically takes 1-5 business days through Georgia's House of Justice. We handle the entire process including name reservation, document preparation, and registration filing. Additional licenses or permits may require additional time.

Do I need a Georgian partner to establish a business? +

No, foreign investors can establish 100% foreign-owned businesses in Georgia. However, having local representation for ongoing compliance and operations can be beneficial. We can serve as your registered agent and provide ongoing corporate support.

What are the ongoing compliance requirements for Georgian companies? +

Companies must file annual declarations, maintain proper corporate records, and comply with tax obligations. Public companies have additional reporting requirements. We provide comprehensive compliance services to ensure your business meets all regulatory obligations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners buy property in Georgia? +

Yes, foreigners can purchase most types of real estate in Georgia, including apartments, houses, and commercial properties. Some restrictions apply to agricultural land and border areas. We guide foreign buyers through the entire purchase process and ensure compliance with all regulations.

What is the property purchase process in Georgia? +

The process involves property verification, due diligence, purchase agreement negotiation, and registration at the House of Justice. We handle title searches, contract preparation, and coordinate with all parties to ensure a smooth transaction. The entire process typically takes 2-4 weeks.

What are the taxes and fees for property transactions? +

Property registration fees are typically 0.5% of the property value. Additional costs may include legal fees, notarization, and translation services. We provide transparent cost estimates and help optimize your tax position throughout the transaction.

Do I need title insurance for Georgian property? +

While not mandatory, title insurance is recommended for significant investments. We conduct comprehensive title searches and due diligence to identify any potential issues before purchase. This helps protect your investment and ensures clear ownership rights.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do commercial disputes typically take to resolve? +

Commercial disputes vary significantly in complexity. Simple contract disputes may resolve in 6-12 months, while complex commercial litigation can take 1-3 years. We always explore settlement opportunities and alternative dispute resolution to expedite resolution when beneficial.

Is arbitration better than court litigation? +

Arbitration offers advantages including confidentiality, specialized arbitrators, and potentially faster resolution. However, court litigation may be preferable for complex legal issues or when precedent is important. We evaluate each case to recommend the optimal dispute resolution strategy.

What are the costs involved in litigation? +

Costs include court fees, attorney fees, expert witness fees, and discovery expenses. We provide transparent fee structures and cost estimates upfront. Many cases can be handled on contingency or alternative fee arrangements depending on the circumstances.

Can Georgian court judgments be enforced internationally? +

Yes, Georgian court judgments can often be enforced in other countries through various international agreements and treaties. The enforceability depends on the specific jurisdiction and applicable treaties. We have experience with cross-border enforcement proceedings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the tax benefits for businesses in Georgia? +

Georgia offers several tax benefits including the Small Business Status (1% tax rate), International IT Company Status (0% tax on foreign profits), and various industry-specific incentives. We help you structure your business to maximize available tax advantages while maintaining compliance.

How does the Small Business Status work in Georgia? +

Small Business Status allows companies with annual turnover under 500,000 GEL to pay only 1% tax instead of standard corporate tax rates. Specific requirements and restrictions apply. We evaluate your eligibility and help maintain compliance with all Small Business Status requirements.

What regulatory requirements apply to international companies? +

International companies must comply with reporting requirements, transfer pricing rules, and various regulatory frameworks depending on their activities. We provide comprehensive compliance services and help navigate complex international regulatory requirements.

How can I resolve disputes with tax authorities? +

Tax disputes can be resolved through administrative appeals, court proceedings, or settlement negotiations. We represent clients throughout the dispute resolution process, from initial audit defense through appeals and litigation if necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take in Georgia? +

Trademark registration typically takes 6-12 months from application to registration, assuming no objections or oppositions. We conduct comprehensive trademark searches before filing to minimize potential conflicts and expedite the registration process.

What can be protected under Georgian intellectual property law? +

Georgian IP law protects trademarks, copyrights, patents, industrial designs, and trade secrets. Each type of IP has specific requirements and protection periods. We help determine the best protection strategy for your intellectual property assets.

Do I need to register my copyright in Georgia? +

Copyright protection exists automatically upon creation of original works, but registration provides additional legal advantages including enhanced enforcement rights and statutory damages. We recommend registration for commercially valuable creative works.

How do I enforce my intellectual property rights? +

IP enforcement options include cease and desist letters, opposition proceedings, civil litigation, and customs enforcement. We develop comprehensive enforcement strategies tailored to your specific IP rights and business objectives, from initial infringement analysis through resolution.
