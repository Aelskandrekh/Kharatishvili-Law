Aleksandre Kharatishvili

Education: LL.M. from Loyola University New Orleans (summa cum laude) and LL.B. from Tbilisi State University (With Honours)
Bar Admissions: New York State Bar and Georgian Bar Association
Experience: 5+ years of practice in corporate, commercial, IP, immigration and dispute resolution
